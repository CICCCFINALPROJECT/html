About Us

Vera’s Burger Shack began as a beach concession at Dundarave Pier in West Vancouver in 1977 under the watchful eye of Vera and her husband Frank. Vera prided herself on serving fresh, delicious, homemade food!

We serve an honest burger. We believe it’s a big part of why we’ve consistently won “Best Burger” awards for 10 years in a row and counting. Thanks for being a part of Vera’s story so far, and for helping us live up to our cheeky slogan -
“You Can't Beat Vera's Meat!”
